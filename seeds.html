Balsum seeds

Impatiens balsamina, commonly known as balsam, garden balsam, rose balsam, touch-me-not or spotted snapweed, is a species of plant native to India and Myanmar.


Lotus seeds

Lotus is the national flower of both India and Vietnam. Interestingly, it is the Lotus pink flowers that are a national symbol


Marigold seeds

Marigolds (Tagetes species) are easy to grow, economical, bloom reliably all summer, and have few insect and disease problems.


Dahlia seeds

Dahlias are tuberous perennials, and most have simple leaves that are segmented and toothed or cut. The compound flowers may be white, yellow, red, or purple in colour.


Tomato seeds

They are usually red, scarlet, or yellow, though green and purple varieties do exist, and they vary in shape from almost spherical to oval and elongate to pear-shaped.


Zinnia seeds

Zinnias are annuals, shrubs, and sub-shrubs native primarily to North America, with a few species in South America.
