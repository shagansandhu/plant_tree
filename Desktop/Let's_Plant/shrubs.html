SHRUBS

ARALIA

Polyscias or Aralia, is a perennial evergreen shrub
or dwarf tree native to India.The species and varieties
of Polyscias are useful foliage plants suitable for growing
in semi shade in ground and in pot.
Botanical Name : Fatsia japonica
Family : Araliaceae
Size : 6-8 ft. tall, 2–3 ft. wide

CROTON

Croton is a diverse and complex taxonomic group of
plants ranging from herbs and shrubs to trees.It was
first mentioned in European literature by Cristóbal
Acosta in 1578 as lignum pavanae.
Botanical Name : Codiaeum variegatum
Family : spurge
Size : 3-8 ft. tall, 3-6 ft. wide

BLACK KODIA

A very striking deep purple colour. Plants make a
fantastic contrast with other light green or yellow
coloured foliage. They are excellent for use in full sunlight.
Botanical Name : Eranthemum nigrum
Family : Amaranthaceae
Size : 1-2 mt. tall, 150 cm wide

SCHEFFLERA

Schefflera also known as umbrella tree houseplant
is a popular plant and comes in many varieties. This
is a large evergreen shrub with glossy leaves.
Botanical Name : Schefflera arboricola (Hayata) Merr.
Family : Araliaceae
Size : 16 in. tall, 5 in. wide

DURANTA GOLDEN

Duranta golden also known as Golden Dewdrop,
Pigeon Berry, and Skyflower, is a species of flowering
shrub in the verbena family Verbenaceae. This is a
vigorous large broadleaf evergreen shrub.
Botanical Name : Duranta erecta
Family : verbena
Size : 2-4 ft. tall, 1-2 ft. wide

BLUEBERRY

They are an upright bush with a relatively shallow,
fibrous root system and woody canes. Fruit is borne
on buds formed during the previous growing season
in late summer.
Botanical Name : Vaccinium caesariense
Family : Ericaceae
Size : 1–8 ft. tall, 2-10 ft. wide
